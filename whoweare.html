Meet Your Team

Nigel Gurusaransingh

Owner & Creative Director A seasoned and standout event professional in the Caribbean Events industry, Nigel Gurusaransingh puts experience at the center of what he does. From his polished and no-drama approach to planning events to a deep appreciation for hosting anyone who’s ready to chat over a cappuccino (or two), one thing is for sure—Nigel is dedicated to a process built on integrity and service excellence. His heartfelt desire to create magic for his clients has earned ARM Maven Contracting shining reviews.


Shanelle Glasgow

Event Producer Shanelle’s affinity for event planning began in 2008, coordinating corporate events at Harvard University. A few years later, she moved to Trinidad and Tobago and has enjoyed event planning in the Caribbean ever since. Over the past 10 years, she has collaborated with clients to make their dreams come true, whether that be an intimate elopement overlooking the Ocean, or large-scale ballroom events.


Renata Sankar

Senior Event Producer Since joining in 2014, Renata’s gifts of organization, concept design, and contagious enthusiasm have earned her an ever-increasing role in planning and coordinating numerous ARM Maven Contracting events. As part of our Senior Team, Renata plays a vital role in the direction and strategic planning within the company. Combining her unique eye for design, over 8 years of wedding coordination, and experience in venue and catering management, Renata knows how to plan and execute truly unforgettable events. She strives to bring her clients’ visions to life and make their event day as fun and worry-free as possible.
